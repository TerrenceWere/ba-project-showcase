# Business Case: Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Executive Summary
**Problem**: Sales leadership lacked timely access to performance data, resulting in 2-week lag in decision-making and missed regional opportunities.

**Recommended Solution**: Centralized sales analytics dashboard with real-time data integration and interactive reporting capabilities.

**Investment**: $85,000 | **ROI**: 240% | **Payback Period**: 6 months

## Business Problem Analysis
### Current State Challenges
- Manual Excel-based reporting consuming 20+ hours weekly
- Data inconsistencies across regions causing reporting conflicts
- 2-week delay in performance visibility impacting tactical decisions
- Limited self-service capabilities for sales team members

### Impact Assessment
- **Financial**: Estimated $200K in missed revenue opportunities annually
- **Operational**: 35% of sales manager time spent on data gathering vs. coaching
- **Strategic**: Inability to respond quickly to market changes and competitor moves

## Solution Options Analysis

### Option 1: Enhanced Power BI Dashboard (RECOMMENDED)
- **Cost**: $85,000
- **Timeline**: 3 months
- **Features**: Real-time data, interactive visuals, mobile access
- **Benefits**: 80% reduction in manual reporting, real-time decision support

### Option 2: Custom-Built Solution
- **Cost**: $150,000
- **Timeline**: 6 months
- **Features**: Fully customized, advanced analytics
- **Benefits**: Complete customization, but longer timeline and higher cost

### Option 3: Do Nothing
- **Cost**: $0
- **Impact**: Continued revenue leakage and operational inefficiency
- **Risk**: Falling behind competitors investing in sales analytics

## Financial Analysis
### Cost Breakdown
| Category | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Power BI Premium | $15,000 |
| Data Engineering | $45,000 |
| Business Analysis | $15,000 |
| Training & Change Management | $10,000 |
| **Total** | **$85,000** |

### Benefit Quantification
| Benefit | Annual Value |
|---------|-------------|
| Revenue increase from better decisions | $120,000 |
| Time savings (20 hours/week × $50/hour) | $52,000 |
| Reduced errors and rework | $28,000 |
| **Total Annual Benefits** | **$200,000** |

### ROI Calculation
- **Net Annual Benefit**: $200,000 - $15,000 (ongoing costs) = $185,000
- **ROI**: ($185,000 / $85,000) × 100 = **218%**
- **Payback Period**: 6 months

## Risk Assessment
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Data quality issues | Medium | High | Data validation framework, phased rollout |
| User adoption resistance | Medium | Medium | Change management plan, super user program |
| Technical integration challenges | Low | Medium | Proof of concept, experienced implementation partner |

## Recommendation
**Approve Option 1** - Enhanced Power BI Dashboard. This solution provides the optimal balance of functionality, cost, and implementation timeline while delivering significant business value.
