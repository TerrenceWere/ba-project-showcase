## Power/Interest Grid Analysis

### Manage Closely (High Power, High Interest)
- **Sarah Chen (VP Sales)**: Project sponsor, final decision authority
- **Mike Johnson (Sales Director)**: Key business owner, daily operations impact

### Keep Satisfied (High Power, Low Interest)  
- **Finance Team**: Budget approval, ROI validation
- **IT Department**: Technical implementation, infrastructure

### Keep Informed (Low Power, High Interest)
- **Regional Managers**: Directly impacted by reporting capabilities
- **Sales Representatives**: Primary users, adoption critical

## Engagement Strategy

### Executive Stakeholders (Sarah Chen, Mike Johnson)
**Communication Frequency**: Weekly
**Methods**: Executive summaries, decision briefs, steering committee
**Key Messages**: Progress against objectives, ROI realization, strategic alignment

### Technical Stakeholders (IT Department)
**Communication Frequency**: Bi-weekly  
**Methods**: Technical reviews, architecture diagrams, integration planning
**Key Messages**: Technical requirements, implementation timeline, dependency management

### Business Users (Regional Managers, Sales Reps)
**Communication Frequency**: Monthly
**Methods**: Demo sessions, feedback workshops, training
**Key Messages**: Feature updates, user benefits, training availability

## Stakeholder Concerns & Mitigation

### VP Sales Concerns
- **Concern**: Will this actually improve sales performance?
- **Mitigation**: Clear success metrics, pilot program with measurable results
- **Engagement**: Regular showcase of progress against KPIs

### Sales Representatives Concerns  
- **Concern**: Another tool to learn, more data entry
- **Mitigation**: Focus on time savings, intuitive design, comprehensive training
- **Engagement**: Involve in design sessions, super user program

### IT Department Concerns
- **Concern**: Data security, system performance, maintenance overhead
- **Mitigation**: Security review, performance testing, clear support model
- **Engagement**: Early technical collaboration, joint solution design

## Change Management Approach

### Awareness & Desire
- **Communicate** the "what's in it for me" for each stakeholder group
- **Showcase** success stories from pilot users
- **Address** concerns proactively through targeted messaging

### Knowledge & Ability  
- **Training**: Role-specific training sessions
- **Documentation**: Quick reference guides and video tutorials
- **Support**: Dedicated super users and help desk

### Reinforcement
- **Recognition**: Celebrate early adopters and success stories
- **Metrics**: Track and communicate adoption and benefit realization
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular feedback loops for enhancements

## Success Metrics for Stakeholder Engagement

- **Executive Satisfaction**: 90%+ satisfaction in steering committee surveys
- **User Adoption**: 80%+ active usage within first month
- **Training Effectiveness**: 85%+ confidence scores post-training
- **Issue Resolution**: 95% of concerns addressed within 48 hours
